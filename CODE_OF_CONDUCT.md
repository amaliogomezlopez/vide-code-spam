# Code of conduct

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive and harassment-free community.
Treat contributors with empathy, discuss ideas rather than people, accept
constructive feedback and avoid discriminatory, threatening or disruptive
behavior.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include harassment, discriminatory language,
personal attacks, publishing private information, sexualized attention and
deliberate disruption of project spaces.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently
restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its community.
Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer contact published on
the GitHub profile. Do not use a public issue. Reports will be reviewed fairly,
confidentially and without retaliation. This policy applies to repository,
issue, pull-request, discussion and project-representative spaces.
